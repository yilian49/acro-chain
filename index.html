Acronym Chain

Link acronyms, clear the board

How to Play

Goal: Clear all letters from the pool by chaining acronyms together.

Turn 1: Enter any real acronym whose first letter and last letter are both in the letter pool. Both letters are removed.

Turns 2+: Your acronym must start with the last letter of the previous acronym. Its last letter must be an unused letter still in the pool. That letter is then removed.

Win: Clear every letter from the pool!

Custom Acronyms: If your acronym isn't in the dictionary, you can submit it with the full phrase and an AI judge will decide if it counts!

Tip: Plan ahead — as the pool shrinks, your options narrow. Think about which ending letters keep your chain alive!
